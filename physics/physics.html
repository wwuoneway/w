Working page at Fermilab

Beam:

  • NuMI:
    • NuMI beam is created from 120 GeV protons hitting a carbon target.
    • NuMI has a higher beam intrinsic electron neutrino component than BNB (5% vs. 0.5%).
    • MicroBooNE is NuMI off-axis, but still receive ~ 2500 electron neutrino events per year.
  • BNB:
    • BNB is created from 8 GeV protons on a beryllium target.
    • MicroBooNE detector is located at 470 m downstream fro the production target of the BNB.

Multiple Coulomb Scattering (MCS):

  • Multiple Coulomb scattering occurs when a charged particle traverses a medium and undergoes electromagnetic scattering off atomic nuclei.
  • The scattering perturbs the original trajectory of particle within the material. This can be discussed by Highland formula.
  • With the highland formula, the momentum of a track-like particle can be determined using only the 3D reconstructed track information, without any calorimetric or track range information.
